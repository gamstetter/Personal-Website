Activities and Leadership

UC CubeCats

Treasurer

As treasurer, I'm responsible for all funding, budgeting and branding that goes into successfully launching a CubeSat. My resonsibilities include:

  • Overseeing the Project Funding and Marketing committees
  • Managing club budgets and affairs with the University Funding Board
  • Analyzing the best products and fundraisers to invest in

I have developed on many of my soft skills, such as public speaking, communication, and time management, through my role as treasurer.

High Altitude Balloon (HAB)

I participated on the software team for the first CubeCats HAB mission. My involvement was with as follows:

  • Learning C++ quickly to allow communication between a raspberryPi and other sensors
  • Creating a parallel processing procedure to survey atmospheric conditions and act accordingly

This experience provided me with skills in C++ and sparked my interest in the aerospace industry.

Association on Computing Machinery - Committee on Women (ACM-W)

Communtiy Service Chair

My role in this organization encompasses all community service related to outreach and support of minority groups in computer science. My resonsibilities include:

  • Collaborating with Microsoft to host DigiGirlz event and introduce young girls to CS
  • Expanding the Google igniteCS volunteer program to reach out to underprivileged high school students
  • Working with similar student organizations to manage campus tutoring program

Mentor

My first year with ACM-W, I participated as a mentor and tutor for underprivileged high school students at Hughes High School. My responsibilities included:

  • Sparking interest in CS among minority students
  • Acting as a project manager to support students with goal-setting, time management and study techniques
  • Providing students with necessary materials for success in the classroom

Cincinnati Symphony Orchestra (CSO)

Concert Enhancer

As concert enhancer, I engage patrons of the CSO in order to give them the best experience around classical music as possible. My responsibilities include:

  • Assisting with way-finding for patrons
  • Operating the gift shop cash register to support the organization's industry obligations
  • Introducing violin to children during music labs to foster their intellectual and emotional growth
